<h2 style="color:#4B0082; font-family:'Trebuchet MS';"> Overview</h2>
<p style="font-size:15px; color:#333; font-family:'Georgia';">
In <b>Milestone 3</b>, I developed an <b>interactive Power BI dashboard</b> to visualize customer insights and performance metrics of food brands.  
The dashboard highlights customer behavior, product performance, and satisfaction levels through well-designed data visualizations and calculated metrics using <b>DAX queries</b>.
</p>



<h2 style="color:#1E90FF; font-family:'Trebuchet MS';"> Key Metrics Implemented (Using DAX)</h2>
<ul style="font-size:15px; color:#333; font-family:'Georgia';">
  <li><b>Average Rating:</b> Calculates the mean customer rating across all food items.</li>
  <li><b>Net Promoter Score (NPS):</b> Derived using DAX to measure customer loyalty and satisfaction.</li>
  <li><b>Most Repeated Item:</b> Identifies the most frequently purchased or reviewed food item.</li>
  <li><b>Correlation between Age and Sentiment Score:</b> Measures how customer age influences feedback sentiment.</li>
  <li><b>Top Selling Items by Region:</b> Displays regional performance across East, North, Metro, South, and West.</li>
  <li><b>Average Rating by Feature and Cuisine Preference:</b> Compares how taste, packaging, quality, and price vary by cuisine type.</li>
</ul>
